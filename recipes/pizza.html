Pizza

Where hearty meets sweet, these indulgent pancakes
designed to satisfy the hunger of champions. Packed
with bold flavors and robust ingredients,
Mancakes redefine breakfast with a manly twist.

ingredients

  • Maple Syrup
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Jalapeños

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy, then crumble or chop it into small pieces
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the pancake batter with shredded cheddar cheese and diced jalapeños for a spicy kick.
  3. Heat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray.
  4. Pour the pancake batter onto the griddle and sprinkle the crumbled bacon on top. Cook until bubbles form on the surface.
